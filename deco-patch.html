    Eco-Conscious Opulence

    Deco Patch

    Deco Patch transforms discarded liquor bottles into objects of refined luxury, giving waste a second life. Through meticulous hand-applied patchwork of metallic foils, textured papers, and artisanal motifs, each empty bottle is upcycled in an eco-conscious way that honors sustainability without compromising style. The results are luminous table lamps, vases etc that cast a warm, mosaic glow, sculptural vases with a jewel-toned presence, and statement showpieces that elevate any interior. No two pieces are alike, making every creation a collectible heirloom. Deco Patch merges green innovation with couture craftsmanship — proving that responsible design can still be opulent, radiant, and deeply personal.
